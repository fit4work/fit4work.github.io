How does it work?

FitForWork takes the guesswork out of equation by performing three simple tests to provide you with accurate information on whether you're fit for work:

  • Fatigue - FitForWork assesses your fatigue and sleep habits
  • Medication Interactions - FitForWork reviews your medication to ensure it doesnt impact your ability to work.
  • Mental Wealth - FitForWork checks you mental wellbeing
FitForWork provides you with direct access to trained medical professionals should you want to discuss things further.

Private. Accurate. Connected

See why people love FitForWork

  • Self Assessments

    FitForWork allows you to self assess your Fatigue, Medication and Mental Wealth.

  • Medication Interactions

    FitForWork automatically reviews the side effects the and interactions with other drugs.

  • Historical Data

    FitForWork records your answers locally allowing you to review your data over time.

  • One Score

    FitForWork provides you with one simple score and risk rating to assess your fitness for work.

  • Access to Medical Professionals

    FitForWork provides you with confidential access and real-time video calls with trained medical professionals if you want to discuss things further.

  • Manager Notification

    FitForWork allows you to share your summary score, not the detail, with your manager should you choose to.

For Companies

FitForWork arms your people with data about their fitness for work before they enter the work place.

FitForWork presents 3 integrated tests and gives your people simple easy to use information so they can assess their own fitness for work. Better informing their leader with any risks associated with themselves without sharing confidential information. For companies this means more informed & empowered people and a safer work place.

For Suppliers

For Medical and Health Care Professionals - Fit For Work connects you to people who need help when they need help. Working together to keep people safe.

You may of become a health care professional to help people. Fit For Work connects you with people who need your help when they need it and they come armed with information that will help you help them more quickly. FitForWork offers appointment scheduling, private video calls and phone consultations with people in need as well as managers who are unclear on what action to take next.
